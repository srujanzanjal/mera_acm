Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our chapter

How can I join the ACM Student Chapter?

To join our chapter, you need to be a student at SVPCET. Fill out our membership form, attend our orientation session, and participate in our activities. We welcome students from all years and departments who are passionate about technology.

What are the benefits of joining ACM?

Members get access to exclusive workshops, networking opportunities, industry visits, technical resources, mentorship programs, and the chance to participate in national and international ACM events. You'll also develop leadership skills and build a strong professional network.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, there is a nominal annual membership fee that covers event costs, resources, and chapter activities. The fee structure is student-friendly and provides excellent value for the benefits offered. Contact us for current fee details.

What kind of events do you organize?

We organize technical workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, industry visits, guest lectures, seminars on emerging technologies, career guidance sessions, and networking events. Check our events page for upcoming activities.

Can I contribute as a volunteer?

We always welcome volunteers for our events and activities. Volunteering is a great way to gain experience, develop skills, and contribute to the tech community. Contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.

How can I stay updated with chapter activities?

Follow us on our social media channels, subscribe to our newsletter, check our website regularly, and join our WhatsApp group for members. We also send email updates about important events and opportunities.
