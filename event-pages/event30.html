About Convincing Power Challenge

The Convincing Power Challenge workshop is a hands-on session designed to provide participants with an in-depth understanding of persuasive communication techniques. The workshop will guide attendees through the essentials of effective messaging, strategies for influencing others, and methods for enhancing their persuasive skills. By combining theoretical knowledge with practical exercises, students will learn how to craft compelling arguments and explore real-world applications, including negotiation and public speaking. The session concludes with an interactive quiz to reinforce learning outcomes in a fun and engaging way.

Event Objective

The objective of this session is to introduce participants to the fundamentals of persuasive communication and help them develop the skills needed to influence and inspire action with confidence. It also aims to provide insights into how different persuasive techniques work and their various applications. Through interactive learning methods such as practical examples, participants will engage more deeply with the material. Additionally, the session will demonstrate how persuasive communication can be applied in real-world scenarios, such as building rapport and negotiating effectively.

Event Highlights

  • Students pitch a product or project to a panel, testing persuasive communication.
  • Focus on creativity, innovation, and confidence in presenting.
  • Mimics real-world pitching scenarios like business/start-up presentations.
  • Judging criteria: Clarity, Convincing Power, Creativity, Confidence/Body Language, Q&A handling.
  • Winners receive certificates.

Result

Skills Covered

  • Persuasive Communication – structuring a convincing pitch.
  • Creativity & Innovation – unique ideas and engaging delivery.
  • Public Speaking & Confidence – body language, tone, eye contact.
  • Teamwork & Structured Thinking – organizing and presenting ideas logically.
  • Critical Thinking & Q&A Handling – addressing tough questions under pressure.
  • Pitching & Presentation Skills – preparing for start-up/investor-like settings.
