About the Event

ConvoSphere 2025, organized by the Department of Computer Engineering and the ACM Student Chapter on January 31, 2025, was a dynamic debate event designed to foster critical thinking, logical reasoning, and effective communication. Targeted at 2nd-year CE students from Section B, the event challenged participants to analyze emerging technological trends and their societal impacts through structured debates.

Coordinated by Prof. Kavita Meshram and student coordinators, the event featured two engaging rounds: Fact or Fiction and Tech Reality Showdown. Participants showcased their argumentation skills, with judges crowning the winning team as the Champion of ConvoSphere 2025 based on argument strength, critical thinking, and engagement.
