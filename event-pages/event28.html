About Technic

Technic was a technical fun event organized by the St. Vincent Pallotti CE ACM Student Chapter, involving 125 students from BVOC, first year, and third-year branches. The event featured three eliminator rounds designed to test technical skills and creativity.

The rounds included a multiplayer drawing guessing game, an online logo identification challenge, and a final scratch programming round.
