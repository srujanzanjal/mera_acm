About Photoshop Workshop

The ACM Chapter organized a two-day Photoshop Workshop for second-year Computer Engineering students. Conducted by Ms. Janhavi Upadhaye, the workshop covered the basics of Photoshop, including various tools and layering concepts.

Section B attended on Day 1, and Section A on Day 2, with a poster-making assignment on “Automated Future” due by April 24th.
