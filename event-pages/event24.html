Speak & Spark 2025 (Section B)

Event Completed

Debate and product pitching competition showcasing creativity, confidence, and collaborative spirit

Date July 25, 2025
Time 2:15 PM – 4:15 PM
Location CE Department
Category Competition

About the Event

Speak & Spark 2025 was a dynamic communication competition designed to challenge participants' oratory skills, critical thinking, and creative presentation abilities. The event featured two exciting rounds that tested different aspects of communication - structured debate and innovative product pitching.

Participants showcased their ability to construct persuasive arguments, think on their feet, and present innovative ideas with confidence. The competition emphasized not only individual speaking skills but also teamwork and collaborative problem-solving.

Competition Rounds

Debate Round

Structured debate on contemporary topics testing argumentation skills.

Debate Format:

  • British Parliamentary style debate
  • Impromptu topic announcement
  • Limited preparation time
  • Constructive speeches and rebuttals
  • Emphasis on logical reasoning
  • Evidence-based arguments

Pitch Me a Product

Innovative product pitching challenge focusing on creativity and presentation.

Pitching Elements:

  • Product concept development
  • Market need identification
  • Value proposition articulation
  • Presentation skills assessment
  • Audience engagement techniques
  • Q&A handling ability
