Category Competition

About the Competition

The Poster Making Competition was a digital design event that challenged participants to create visually compelling posters using Canva, a popular online design tool. The competition provided a platform for students to showcase their creativity, digital artistry, and visual communication skills.

Participants worked with diverse themes ranging from patriotic concepts like Independence Day to technology-inspired ideas, allowing for a wide expression of creativity and innovation. The online format made the competition accessible to all students, regardless of their physical location.

Patriotic designs celebrating India's independence and national pride.
