About CODE-DECODE

The Department of Computer Engineering, in collaboration with the ACM CE Student Chapter, successfully conducted the event < CODE - DECODE> on 18th and 20th August 2025 for 2nd Year CE students (Sections A & B). The event focused on sharpening participants’ logical reasoning and code debugging abilities. Working in teams, students solved programming challenges using languages of their choice — C, C++, Java, or Python — hosted on the HackerRank platform. Each session was held for 1 hour (3:15 PM to 4:15 PM) at the CE Department. The event saw enthusiastic participation and fostered a spirit of collaboration, problem-solving, and technical growth.

Event Objective

Strengthen foundational programming concepts. Encourage teamwork and peer learning. Provide hands-on experience with competitive coding platforms. Promote the use of multiple programming languages (C, C++, Java, Python). Foster a spirit of analytical thinking and technical excellence among budding engineers.
