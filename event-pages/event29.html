About Prompt Engineering

The Prompt Engineering Workshop is a hands-on session designed to provide participants with an in-depth understanding of prompt engineering techniques for Large Language Models (LLMs). The workshop will guide attendees through the essentials of effective prompt writing, strategies for optimizing AI interactions, and methods for enhancing model performance. By combining theoretical knowledge with practical exercises, students will learn how to design impactful prompts and explore real-world applications, including the creation of AI-powered chatbots. The session concludes with an interactive quiz to reinforce learning outcomes in a fun and engaging way.

Event Objective

The objective of this session is to introduce participants to the fundamentals of prompt engineering and help them develop the skills needed to write effective prompts that optimize AI responses. It also aims to provide insights into how different large language models (LLMs) work and their various applications. Through interactive learning methods such as practical examples and quizzes, participants will engage more deeply with the material. Additionally, the session will demonstrate how prompt engineering can be applied in real-world scenarios, such as building AI chatbots.

Event Highlights

  • Introduction to Prompt Engineering.
  • Benefits of Prompt Engineering in AI applications.
  • Key parameters of effective prompt writing.
  • The “4 S” framework for writing powerful prompts.
  • Model-based working of different Large Language Models (LLMs).
  • Practical demo on chatbot creation using prompting and ChatGPT.
  • Interactive quiz session for participants.
