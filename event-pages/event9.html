About Cloud Carnival

The Cloud Carnival, hosted by the St. Vincent CE ACM Student Chapter, was a successful event featuring an inspiring inauguration and two engaging rounds: a multiple-choice quiz on cloud computing and a task-oriented challenge.

It showcased students' knowledge and skills, with active participation from various departments.
