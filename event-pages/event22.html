About Salesforce Workshop

The Department of Computer Engineering, in collaboration with the ACM Student Chapter, organized a hands-on Salesforce workshop on 2nd August 2025. The session was led by Mr. Sanjay Khajure, Senior Salesforce Developer at MindzCloud Technologies, Nagpur, and was attended by 60 enthusiastic 2nd and 3rd-year students. Spanning four hours (11:00 AM – 4:00 PM), the workshop provided in-depth insights into the Salesforce ecosystem, including UI navigation, data modeling, workflows, automation using Process Builder, and an overview of career opportunities in Salesforce. The event was smoothly coordinated by Prof. Yogita Nikhare, Dr. Pallavi Wankhede, and student coordinators Avantee Sarve (CSBS), Amiya Bose (CE), along with the ACM student team.

Event Objective

Introduce students to Salesforce UI and navigation. Explain core concepts such as data modeling, workflows, and automation using Process Builder. Provide hands-on exposure to Salesforce tools and features. Highlight real-world use cases and best practices in Salesforce development. Create awareness about career paths and opportunities within the Salesforce domain. Bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry-relevant skills.
